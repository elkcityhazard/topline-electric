 Industrial Electricians

Industrial buildings today have more electrical requirements than ever before in construction history. As experienced industrial electricians, many Traverse City-Northern Michigan business rely on our expertise for timely and professional electrical services. See our Commercial Electrical Services for a complete listing of our services from construction to maintenance.

Our industrial electrical services include:

  • New construction
  • Remodel & retrofits
  • Troubleshooting
  • Data cabling
  • Design Build Service
  • Equipment Power
  • Security Lighting
  • Fire Alarm Systems
  • Transformers
  • Heat Trace
  • Door Access System
  • Machinery Relocations
  • Exit signs & egress lights
